# 5.4 Wireframe Diagram – Instructions

## Purpose
Draw **wireframe diagrams** (low-fidelity UI layouts) for the main screens of the Smart Healthcare System. Wireframes show structure and placement, not final colors or images.

---

## Screens to Wireframe

### 1. **Login Page**
- **Elements:** Logo/App name, Email input, Password input, “Login” button, “Register” link (for new patients).
- **Layout:** Centered card or form; optional “Patient / Doctor / Admin” note (or single login that routes by role).

### 2. **Patient Dashboard**
- **Elements:** Header (welcome, logout), “High Risk Alert” banner (if any), Stats cards (Total Predictions, High Risk, Reviewed), Quick actions (Diabetes / Heart / Kidney prediction links), Recent predictions list, Link to Profile and History.
- **Layout:** Top header; below it stats in a row; then quick action cards; then list or table.

### 3. **Prediction Form (e.g. Diabetes)**
- **Elements:** Page title (“Diabetes Risk Prediction”), Form fields (as per backend: e.g. Pregnancies, Glucose, BloodPressure, etc.), “Submit” / “Predict” button, Back link to dashboard.
- **Layout:** Single column form; labels above or beside inputs; button at bottom.

### 4. **Prediction Result**
- **Elements:** Disease name, “Disease: Yes / No”, Risk level (e.g. High – with visual/color cue), Risk percentage, Recommendation text (paragraph), YouTube video embed (rectangle placeholder), “Back to Dashboard” or “New Prediction”.
- **Layout:** Result block on top; recommendation below; video embed below that.

### 5. **Prediction History**
- **Elements:** Title “My Predictions”, Filter (e.g. by disease: All / Diabetes / Heart / Kidney), “New Prediction” button, List of past predictions (disease type, date, risk %, risk level), Optional “High Risk” section at top.
- **Layout:** Filter and button in one row; list of cards or rows below.

### 6. **Patient Profile**
- **Elements:** Title “Update Profile”, Fields: Full Name, Email, Phone, Age, Gender, Address, Emergency Contact, Medical History (textarea/list), Allergies, Current Medications, “Save” button, Back link.
- **Layout:** Single form; long page or sections.

### 7. **Doctor Dashboard**
- **Elements:** Header, Stats (e.g. Pending reviews, High-risk count, Patients), Notifications list/bell, Links: Patients, Reviews, Consultations, Recent predictions table.
- **Layout:** Header; stat cards; sidebar or top nav for links; content area.

### 8. **Doctor – Patient Profile (View + Remarks)**
- **Elements:** Patient name, Basic info (email, phone, age, etc.), Medical history / allergies / medications (read-only or summary), List of “Medical Remarks” (doctor name, date, text), “Add Remark” textarea and “Add” button.
- **Layout:** Info section on top; remarks list; add-remark form at bottom.

### 9. **Doctor – Consultations**
- **Elements:** “Schedule New Consultation” button, List of consultations (patient name, date, status), Actions: Mark completed / Cancel; Form (when creating): Patient dropdown, Date/Time, Notes, Submit.
- **Layout:** Button and list; modal or inline form for new consultation.

### 10. **Admin Dashboard**
- **Elements:** Header, Stats cards (Total Users, Doctors, Patients, Predictions), Links: Patients, Doctors, Reports, ML Models, Settings, Alerts.
- **Layout:** Header; stat cards; grid or list of navigation links.

### 11. **Admin – Manage Patients**
- **Elements:** Search box, Table: columns (ID, Name, Email, Phone, Status, Assigned Doctor dropdown, Registered, Actions: Deactivate/Activate, View).
- **Layout:** Full-width table; search at top.

### 12. **Admin – Settings**
- **Elements:** Section “General”: System name, Max file size, Session timeout; Section “Notifications”: Email on/off, SMS on/off; Section “System”: Maintenance mode, Allow registration; “Save Settings” button.
- **Layout:** Sections one below the other; toggles or inputs per setting.

### 13. **Admin – Reports**
- **Elements:** Title “Reports & Analytics”, Cards: Total Patients, Total Doctors, Total Predictions, High-Risk Predictions; Section “Predictions by Disease”: Diabetes, Heart, Kidney counts.
- **Layout:** Top row of stat cards; below that disease-wise blocks.

### 14. **Admin – ML Models**
- **Elements:** Title “ML Models”, Path text, List of file names (e.g. diabetes_model.pkl, threshold.json).
- **Layout:** Simple list or table of filenames.

---

## How to Draw Wireframes

- **Style:** Black/white or grey; use rectangles for buttons and inputs, lines for text, placeholder for images/video.
- **Annotations:** Short labels (e.g. “Email”, “Risk Level – color coded”, “YouTube embed”).
- **One wireframe per screen** (or one page with multiple frames numbered 1–14).
- **Tool:** Draw.io, Figma, Balsamiq, or even paper/pen photographed.
